### AFFiNe | Angling Freshwater Fish Netherlands

30 common freshwater fish species of The Netherlands, photographed by anglers

### Summary
This dataset contains images of freshwater fish in The Netherlands:

- 7.000+ images in total
- 30 different species
- 100-500 images per species
- images grouped in a folder per species, for classification training
- one YOLO-file included per image, for object detection training

### Origin and privacy
Images come from the digital angling platform https://www.vangstenregistratie.nl.
Users have agreed to have their images included.
We have applied the following to minimize risk of including privacy sensitive information:

1. face blurring
2. subject cropping 

### Quality and validation
The dataset is gathered and validated using citizen science efforts.
Therefor, it can and probably will contain some wrongly classified images.
If you have expert feedback on certain images, we would like to hear from you.
Please contact us at https://vaartsoftware.nl.
